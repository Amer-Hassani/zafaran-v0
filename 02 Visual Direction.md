# Zafran — Visual Direction

## Visual mood (one sentence)

Warm, modern, MENA-rooted social commerce — feels like a beautiful Instagram feed, not a SaaS dashboard.

## Color signature

- **Primary:** saffron — deep, vivid, slightly orange-red.
- **Secondary:** amber / honey — warmer, softer, friendly.
- **Neutrals:** cream, sand, off-white — gives breathing room.
- **Anchor dark:** espresso or deep ink — for type and contrast.
- **Optional accent:** fresh green or teal — used sparingly to signal AI enhancement / freshness / "go."
- **Avoid:** neon, electric blue, pure cyber black, generic SaaS purple.

## Typography

- **Pairing requirement:** Arabic + Latin must feel like one family, not two bolted together.
- **Latin direction:** geometric humanist sans (think: Söhne, General Sans, Inter Display) — clean, confident, friendly.
- **Arabic direction:** modern naskh / contemporary Arabic sans (think: TheMix Arabic, RTL Mada, IBM Plex Arabic, Rubik Arabic).
- **Weights:** Display (700), Regular (400), Mono optional for prices/codes.
- **Headline behavior:** large, tight tracking, comfortable with both AR and EN at the same point size.

## Logo direction

- **Wordmark:** "Zafran" in Latin and "زعفران" in Arabic — both equally polished.
- **Monogram:** a single-letter mark (Z / ز) usable as app icon.
- **Symbol concept directions to explore:**
    1. **Saffron thread** — three delicate strands forming a Z or a storefront arch.
    2. **Storefront tile** — a small rounded rectangle (mimicking an Instagram post) opening into a shop.
    3. **Spark + grid** — a single saffron spark transforming a 9-tile grid (Instagram feed → store).

## Iconography

- Soft geometry, rounded corners (2–4px radius), 1.5–2px stroke.
- Categories: commerce (cart, tag, box), social (heart, share, story ring), AI (sparkle, magic wand, gradient dot).
- Avoid: hard tech, circuit lines, brackets, terminal cursors.

## Motif / pattern system

- **Saffron threads** — fine, sweeping curves used as background ornament.
- **Story-ring gradient** — a circular saffron gradient that echoes Instagram story rings.
- **Tile mosaic** — a 3×3 grid of small product tiles, suggesting a feed-turned-storefront.

## Photography & illustration

- **Photography:** real Instagram-native product shots — close-up textures (fabric, food, ceramic, jewelry), human hands, natural light.
- **Avoid:** stock SaaS photography, headset-wearing customer support, abstract "data" imagery.
- **Illustration:** minimal, line-led, saffron-accented. Use sparingly for empty states and onboarding.

## AI visual cues

- Sparkles, gentle radial glow, soft animated gradients.
- Always paired with warm, human imagery — never standalone tech effects.
- Color of "AI moments": saffron + accent mint, never neon or electric.

## Layout and product surface

- **Mobile-first.** The seller will use this on their phone, between IG sessions.
- **Card-based UI** with generous padding and warm neutrals as the dominant surface.
- **Bilingual layout rules:** mirror cleanly in RTL; never auto-flip iconography that has cultural reading direction (e.g., arrows, time icons).

## Tone in motion

- Micro-interactions: short, soft, springy.
- Loading states for AI tasks: a saffron thread weaving across the screen, not a spinner.
- Empty states: warm, encouraging copy + a single illustration.

## Reference brands to study

- **Clarity & confidence:** Linear, Vercel, Stripe.
- **Warm minimalism:** Notion, Cron, Raycast.
- **Color restraint, editorial calm:** Glossier, Aesop.
- **Arabic-first done well:** Anghami, Careem, Tabby, Tamara.
